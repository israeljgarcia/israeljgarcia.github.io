Site Purpose

Get Easy Climate will provide an easy access for weather and relevant stories/images for Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. The weather elements include current temperature, wind chill, and highs and lows for the day as well as a storm center to alert for incoming storms. There will also be a gallery that contains images pertaining to each town. This site is easy to navigate, responsive, and provides the user quick info on what they need.
